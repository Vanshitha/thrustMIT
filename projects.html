Our Projects

P H O E N I X

  • Weight: 36Kgs
  • Diameter: 150 mm
  • Apogee: 3km
  • Payload: Peizo-electric device to detect vibrations
  • Recovery System: in house Reefing cutter
  • Fuselage Material: Carbon Fibre

To be launched in Spaceport America Cup 2021. Carbon fiber airframe and glass fiber avionics bay to facilitate telemetry, Aluminum bulkheads and internal modular support structure for easy assembly and interchangeable bays, Male mould with improved stack up for better interior surface finish. Female split piece mould for couplers, nosecone and boattail. Exterior coating on fuselage to improve outer surface finish for better rocket aerodynamics. Nose tip of Aluminum.

A R Y A

  • Weight: 24Kgs
  • Diameter: 140 mm
  • Apogee: 3km
  • Payload: Non-Newtonian fluid damper
  • Recovery System: In house Reefing cutter
  • Fuselage Material: Carbon Fibre

Arya, launched in spaceport America cup 2019, was made entirely of carbon fibre. In this, we incorporated Al 6061T6 bulkheads, centring rings. Lapwing 2 was a subscale rocket used to test all the system. An in house reefing mechanism was designed to facilitate the easier recovery of the rocket. Male mould for fuselage Female split piece moulds for couplers, nosecone and boattail was used in the manufacturing o the rocket

V Y O M

  • Weight: 26Kgs
  • Diameter: 160 mm
  • Apogee: 1.2km
  • Payload: Non-functioning
  • Recovery System: COTS Reefing cutter
  • Fuselage Material: Fibreglass

Vyom was launched in Spaceport America Cup 2018.Glass fiber airframe and couplers and fin/attachment, Aluminium 6061 T6 bulkheads, centering rings, All female mould layup of GF and split piece moulds for nosecone and boattail, nose tip of ABS.
Lapwing 1 was our subscale launch for Vyom to test ideas and functionality of every subsystem.
